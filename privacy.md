# Privacy Policy

**TutorAid** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use TutorAid.

**Last updated: March 2026**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

TutorAid stores all data **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers.

The data you enter in the app (student names, lesson records, income statistics, teaching materials) remains solely on your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

---

## 2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no access to this data at any time.

---

## 3. Purchases and Subscriptions

TutorAid offers optional paid subscriptions through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## 4. Analytics

TutorAid does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage behavior.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

TutorAid is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
